## How to Use Community Farms

### General Guidelines

1. **Share Resources**: Don't monopolize farms - take what you need and let others use them
2. **Maintain Farms**: Help repair and maintain farms when needed
3. **Report Issues**: Let the community know if a farm is broken or needs attention
4. **Be Respectful**: Don't grief or destroy farm structures

### Farm Etiquette

- **AFK Item Handling**: If you are AFK, you can either leave the items at the farm or move them to Item Vault
- **Don't AFK for Extended Periods**: Let others use the farms too
- **Clean Up**: Don't leave items scattered around farms
- **Ask Questions**: If you're unsure how to use a farm, ask in Discord
- **Contribute**: Help build new farms or improve existing ones

## Farm Coordinates

### Important Locations

- **Home Spawn**: Central hub with Item Sorter, Egg Farm, and Zoo
- **Flower District**: Coordinates available via waypoints
- **Farm District**: Main farming area with most automated farms
- **End Dimension**: Access via End portal - Shulker and Enderman farms
- **Nether**: Access via Nether portal - Gold, Hoglin, Wither Skull, and Blaze farms

### Waypoints

Waypoints are scattered across the world. You will need to make your way to districts by foot for the first time. Ask someone to help you navigate to where you need to go. Once you've discovered the locations, you can use the waypoint system in your map mods to navigate between farms:

- **Spawn Hub**: Central meeting point at Home Spawn
- **Flower District**: Flower, Moss, and Tulip farms
- **Farm District**: Main farming area with extensive automation
- **End Farms**: Shulker and Enderman farms
- **Nether Farms**: Gold, Hoglin, Wither Skull, and Blaze farms
